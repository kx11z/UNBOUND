UNBOUND is a drone education initiative in San Jose focused on making robotics through flight accessible, hands-on, and meaningful.

From DJI Tello programming to FPV drone builds to outreach for hospitalized children, UNBOUND is about bringing engineering and freedom together.

What makes UNBOUND different

🚁 Drone-first

Focused deeply on drones, not general STEM.

🛠️ Hands-on

Build, wire, and fly — not just theory.

✨ Meaningful

Bringing flight to kids who can’t go outside.

About UNBOUND

UNBOUND was created to make drone education accessible in Silicon Valley, where surprisingly few structured opportunities exist.

Drones combine hardware, software, and creativity — but getting started is often unclear. UNBOUND simplifies that.
